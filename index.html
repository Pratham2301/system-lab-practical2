ABOUT US

The RCOEM ALUMNI ASSOCIATION is dedicated to bring together the Alumni fraternity on a common platform to build a bridge of personal and professional support to members through 'self help' within community. The important objectives of the Association are to promote and foster mutual beneficial interaction between the Alumni, present students of RCOEM and between Alumni themselves. The aim is to encourage Alumni to take an active and abiding interest in the work and progress of the Institute so as to contribute towards the enhancement of the social utility of their Alma Mater. It has a vital role to play in the college, which are some of the following:-

  1. Recognizing the college reputation.
  2. Recruitment help with the students' prospective.
  3. Sustaining loyalty among the RCOEM alumni.
  4. Intellectual stimulation.
